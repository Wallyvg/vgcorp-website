Privacy & POPIA Policy

VG Corp (Pty) Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

1. Information Officer

In accordance with POPIA, VG Corp (Pty) Ltd has appointed an Information Officer to oversee data privacy compliance:

  • Information Officer: Wally van Greune
  • Email: wally@vgcorp.co.za

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to process your transactions and provide access to our digital platforms (Nexus, PriceSmart, WildPath). This includes:

  • Name and contact information.
  • Business registration details (where applicable).
  • Payment information processed via secure, PCI-compliant third-party partners.

3. Purpose of Processing

Your data is used solely for:

  • Service delivery and account management.
  • Invoicing and financial record-keeping.
  • Communication regarding service updates or security alerts.

We do not sell, rent, or lease our customer lists to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or destruction. All online payment transactions are processed through encrypted, secure gateways.

5. Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information held by us. Please direct any such requests to our Information Officer.
