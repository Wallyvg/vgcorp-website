Account Deletion

At VG Corp (Pty) Ltd, your privacy and data security are our top priorities. In compliance with POPIA, if you wish to deactivate your account and permanently remove your data from our ecosystem (including WildPath, Nexus, or PriceSmart), please follow the process below.

How to Request Deletion:

To verify your identity and protect your data, please send an email from your registered email address to:

wally@vgcorp.co.za

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Required Info: Your full name and the specific platform account you wish to remove.

Data Retention & Removal:

Once your request is verified (typically within 48–72 hours), the following data will be permanently purged from our secure Firebase environment:

  • User Profile: Identity details, contact numbers, and login credentials.
  • Activity Data: Interaction logs, history, and preferences.
  • Safety Data: Any stored emergency logs or safety contacts.

Notice: This action is irreversible. Once the data is purged, it cannot be recovered.
