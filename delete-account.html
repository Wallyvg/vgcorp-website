WildPath Account Deletion

At VG Corp, we value your privacy. If you wish to delete your WildPath account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below:

How to delete your account:

Please send an email to support@vgcorp.co.za with the following details:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request - WildPath
  • Message: Include your registered email address and name.

What data will be removed?

Upon processing your request (usually within 48 hours), the following data will be permanently deleted from our Firebase servers:

  • User Profile (Name, Email, Phone Number)
  • Personal Booking History
  • SOS Emergency Contacts
