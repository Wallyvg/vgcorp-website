Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Overview

VG Corp (Pty) Ltd manages various brands with different fulfillment models. This policy outlines the general standards for refunds and cancellations across our ecosystem.

2. Digital Subscriptions (e.g., PriceSmart)

Subscribers may cancel their service at any time via their account dashboard. Cancellations will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Because our data products are digital and instantly accessible, we generally do not offer pro-rated refunds for partial months of service.

3. Physical Products (e.g., Aquavra)

In accordance with the South African Consumer Protection Act (CPA), customers have a 7-day "cooling-off" period for products purchased online. Items must be returned in their original, unopened packaging at the customer's expense for a full refund.

4. Travel Bookings (e.g., WildPath)

WildPath acts as a discovery platform. Refund and cancellation terms for specific tours are governed by the individual Tour Operator's policy as displayed at the time of booking.

5. How to Initiate a Refund

Please contact our financial department with your order or subscription details:

Email: wally@vgcorp.co.za
Processing Time: Approved refunds are typically processed within 7-10 business days.
