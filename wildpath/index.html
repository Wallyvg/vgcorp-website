WildPath Platform & Legal Documents

WildPath is a South African travel discovery platform connecting travellers with verified tour operators, accommodations, and experiences. The platform is designed to provide a structured, safe, and transparent travel environment.

Platform Overview

WildPath enables travellers to discover, book, and coordinate experiences with local operators. The platform includes real-time communication, digital voucher verification, and safety features such as GPS-based SOS alerts.

Payments & Revenue Model

All payments are securely processed via PCI-DSS compliant third-party payment providers. WildPath operates on a revenue-sharing model:

• 90% paid to the Operator
• 10% retained as a platform service fee

All transactions are conducted in South African Rand (ZAR).
