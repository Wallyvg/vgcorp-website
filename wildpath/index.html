WildPath

A South African travel and tour discovery platform connecting travellers with verified local operators and authentic experiences.

What is WildPath?

WildPath is a South Africa–focused travel platform designed to simplify how travellers discover, plan, and experience tours, accommodation, attractions, and guided activities across all nine provinces.

The platform bridges the gap between travellers and local operators by providing a trusted, transparent, and safety-focused digital experience.

Project in development

Core Purpose

  • Enable travellers to discover verified tours and experiences
  • Support local operators with digital visibility and coordination tools
  • Improve safety, communication, and trip coordination
  • Promote responsible and authentic South African tourism

Project Scope

  • Traveller discovery and trip coordination
  • Operator dashboards and voucher verification
  • In-app communication and safety features
  • Coverage across all South African provinces

Detailed features, legal agreements, and commercial terms will be published separately as the platform progresses.

Ownership & Structure

WildPath is an independent project owned and managed by VG Corp (Pty) Ltd.

All legal agreements, payment processing, and app store compliance will be handled under WildPath’s own documentation and platforms.
