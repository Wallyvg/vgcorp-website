What is WildPath?

WildPath is a digital travel marketplace that connects travellers with independent tour operators, accommodation providers, and guides across South Africa.

WildPath does not operate tours or accommodation directly. It provides technology, discovery, and secure payment facilitation.

For Travellers

  • Browse all 9 South African provinces
  • Book verified operators and experiences
  • Secure PayFast payments
  • Emergency SOS & safety tools

For Operators

  • List tours, accommodation, and activities
  • Receive 90% of every booking
  • Voucher scanning & check-in
  • Operator dashboard & revenue tracking

Payments

All payments are processed via PayFast. Each transaction is split automatically:

  • 90% paid to the Operator
  • 10% retained by WildPath as a platform service fee
