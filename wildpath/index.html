Platform Overview

WildPath enables travellers to discover, book, and coordinate travel experiences in real time. The platform includes secure payments, digital voucher validation, and integrated safety features such as GPS-based SOS alerts.

Business Model & Role

WildPath operates as a marketplace platform. We facilitate bookings between Travellers and independent Operators.

The service agreement exists directly between the Traveller and the Operator. WildPath is not the provider of travel services.

Payments & Revenue Model

Payments are securely processed via PayFast (PCI-DSS compliant).

• 90% paid to the Operator
• 10% retained by WildPath as a platform service fee

Payments are held securely and released after booking confirmation or voucher redemption.

All transactions are processed in South African Rand (ZAR).

Cancellation & Refund Policy

WildPath applies a structured refund system to balance fairness:

• >48 Hours: 100% refund (excluding transaction fees)
• 24–48 Hours: 50% refund
• <24 Hours / No-show: No refund
• Operator Cancellation: 100% refund
