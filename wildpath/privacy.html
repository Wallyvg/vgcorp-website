WildPath Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

WildPath is operated by VG Corp (Pty) Ltd, a company registered in South Africa (Reg: 2025/519458/07). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity Information: Name, ID/passport (for operator verification only)
  • Contact Information: Email address and phone number
  • Location Data: GPS location for discovery and SOS safety features
  • Usage Data: App interactions, bookings, and activity logs
  • Media: Uploaded images for listings or verification

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Facilitate bookings between travellers and operators
  • Verify operators through the WildPath Safety Gate
  • Process secure payments via PayFast
  • Enable communication between users
  • Provide SOS emergency support functionality

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade personal data. Information is only shared with:

  • Payment processors (PayFast)
  • Cloud infrastructure providers (Firebase / Google Cloud)
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards including encryption in transit and at rest. Access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel only.

5. Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections
  • Request deletion of your data

You may also request deletion via our Account Deletion Page.

6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for service delivery and legal compliance.
