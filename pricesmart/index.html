PriceSmart

Retail Intelligence Platform – South Africa

PriceSmart is a digital platform designed to help South African consumers make smarter purchasing decisions. By aggregating real-time retail pricing data, PriceSmart enables users to compare prices, track spending, and optimize grocery shopping through data-driven insights.

Core Features

• Real-time product pricing across retailers
• Grocery list management and cost tracking
• Location-based specials and deals
• Smart budgeting insights

Platform Availability

PriceSmart is currently in development and will be available as a mobile application.

Payments & Compliance

All transactions within the VG Corp ecosystem are processed securely via PCI-DSS compliant third-party payment providers. All pricing is displayed in South African Rand (ZAR).
