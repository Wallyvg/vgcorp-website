Retail Data Disclaimer

PriceSmart aggregates pricing information from independent South African retailers including Checkers, Pick n Pay, Shoprite, SPAR and Woolworths.

PriceSmart is an independent software platform and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or operated by any retailer.

Prices, promotions and stock availability may vary by province, city, suburb and individual store location. Actual in-store pricing always takes precedence.

Privacy & POPIA Compliance

PriceSmart complies with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

Information collected may include account details, shopping preferences, barcode scans, grocery lists and location settings required to provide localized pricing results.

Personal information is never sold to third-party marketers.
