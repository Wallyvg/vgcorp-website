PriceSmart Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2025

PriceSmart is a South African price-aggregation app operated by VG Corp (Pty) Ltd t/a PriceSmart.

1. Data Collected

  • Account details (email, name)
  • Subscription & payment info (via PayFast or Google Play)
  • Device information (analytics, app usage)

2. Purpose

To provide monthly subscription access, personalise app features, and comply with legal requirements.

3. No Retail Transactions

PriceSmart does not sell products. It only aggregates publicly available pricing from retailers.

4. POPIA Compliance

All data is encrypted and stored securely. Users may request deletion or correction of their data at any time.
