PriceSmart Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

PriceSmart is a grocery price comparison and retail intelligence platform owned and operated by VG Corp (Pty) Ltd (Registration Number: 2025/519458/07).

This Privacy Policy explains how personal information is collected, processed, stored, and protected when using the PriceSmart mobile application and associated services.

VG Corp (Pty) Ltd complies with the Protection of Personal Information Act, No. 4 of 2013 (POPIA) of South Africa.

1. Responsible Party

VG Corp (Pty) Ltd acts as the Responsible Party for all personal information processed through PriceSmart.

Merchant Domicile: Cape Town, South Africa

2. Information We Collect

To provide regional grocery price comparison services, PriceSmart may collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Account authentication credentials
  • Subscription and billing status
  • Province, city and suburb selections
  • Custom grocery lists
  • Product barcode scan information
  • Application usage analytics
  • Device and diagnostic information

3. Purpose of Processing

Personal information is processed strictly for:

  • Providing grocery comparison services
  • Displaying regional retail specials
  • Managing subscriptions and account access
  • Improving application performance
  • Customer support
  • Legal and regulatory compliance

4. Third-Party Services

PriceSmart utilizes trusted third-party infrastructure providers including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Google Cloud Platform
  • Cloud Firestore Database
  • Google Play Billing

Information may be processed through these providers solely to deliver PriceSmart services.

5. Data Sharing

VG Corp (Pty) Ltd does not sell, rent, or lease personal information to advertisers or third-party marketers.

Information is shared only with:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers
  • Subscription billing processors
  • Government authorities where legally required

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organisational safeguards are implemented to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Access to user data is restricted and monitored using industry-standard security practices.

7. Data Retention

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and maintain operational records.

8. Account Deletion & Data Erasure

Users may permanently delete their account and associated information at any time.

Deletion may be requested by:

  • Using the "Delete My Account" option within the application
  • Emailing a deletion request from the registered email address

Deletion requests should be sent to:

wally@vgcorp.co.za

Verified requests are processed within 7 business days.

Once completed, deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

9. Your POPIA Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access personal information held about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Object to processing where legally permitted
  • Lodge complaints with the Information Regulator
