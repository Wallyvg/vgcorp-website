Account Deletion Request

At VG Corp (Pty) Ltd, your privacy and data protection are important to us. In accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and global data safety standards, users may request the absolute deletion of their personal data associated with any VG Corp-related platform.

VG Corp operates as a holding company. Individual platforms such as PriceSmart, Nexus, Aquavra, and specifically our travel application WildPath process user data independently under clear privacy frameworks.

Submit Deletion Request

Use the secure compliance form below to instantly initiate your account and data erasure process.

Alternative Request Method

You can also request manual account deletion directly by sending an email from your registered address to:

wally@vgcorp.co.za

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Required Information: Your full name and the specific platform account to be removed

Data Removal Scope

Once your request is processed and verified (typically completed within 48–72 hours), the following information will be permanently purged from our infrastructure:

  • User profile information (names, securely stored authentication credentials, contact details)
  • Account-related transaction or activity histories
  • Associated application metrics and usage tracking data where applicable

This action is completely irreversible and cannot be recovered.
