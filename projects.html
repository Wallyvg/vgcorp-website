Pricesmart

Pricesmart is a mobile app for South African users that aggregates publicly available specials and prices from multiple retailers, filterable by province, city and suburb. The app is available on Google Play. Subscription: R12/month or R120/year (via Google Play billing).

  • Compare prices across retailers
  • Search specials by product, brand or category
  • Location-based filtering (province, city, suburb)
  • Save favourites and receive alerts (premium)

Website: zalvory.co.za/pricesmart
