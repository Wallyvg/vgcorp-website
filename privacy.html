Privacy & POPIA Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

VG Corp (Pty) Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines our commitment to your privacy under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa. This policy applies to our corporate website and our associated digital ecosystem, including Wildpath, Nexus, and PriceSmart.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our digital automation and travel discovery services, including:

  • Identity Information: Full name and identification numbers for operator/guide verification.
  • Contact Information: Email address and phone numbers.
  • Location Data: GPS location data to enable tour discovery and emergency SOS services within our mobile applications.
  • Financial Information: Payment details processed securely via our PCI-compliant merchant partners.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for service delivery within the VG Corp ecosystem:

  • Verifying the "Safety Gate" for Wildpath Operators.
  • Processing secure transactions for licenses and products.
  • Enabling real-time communication between users and service providers.
  • Providing emergency assistance and fulfillment of orders.

3. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data is shared only with essential service providers (such as hosting and payment gateways) necessary to fulfill our service to you. We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect data at rest and in transit.

4. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Under POPIA, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. Users of our mobile platforms may utilize the "Delete Account" feature to immediately purge identity data from our active databases.
