Wildpath Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

Wildpath ("the App") is a mobile application developed and managed by VG Corp (Pty) Ltd, based in South Africa. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to your privacy under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity Information: Full name, South African ID or Passport number (for Operator/Guide verification only).
  • Contact Information: Email address and phone number.
  • Location Data: Precise GPS location to enable tour discovery and the SOS Emergency Service.
  • Media: Photos uploaded for business galleries or profile verification.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly to provide services within the Wildpath ecosystem, including:

  • Verifying the "Safety Gate" for all Operators.
  • Processing secure payments via PayFast.
  • Enabling real-time communication between Travellers and Guides.
  • Providing emergency assistance via the SOS button.

3. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with essential service providers like Google Firebase (Hosting) and PayFast (Payments). All data is encrypted during transit and at rest.

4. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Under POPIA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Wildpath provides a "Delete Account" feature within the App settings which will immediately purge your identity and profile data from our active Firestore databases.
