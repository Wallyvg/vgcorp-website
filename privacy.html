Privacy & POPIA Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

VG Corp (Pty) Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is a South African holding company committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines our responsibilities under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

VG Corp operates as a holding entity. Individual brands and platforms (including PriceSmart, Zalvory, Aquavra, and WildPath) may collect and process personal data independently under their own privacy policies.

1. Information We Collect

VG Corp collects limited information necessary for corporate and administrative purposes:

  • Name and contact information (where voluntarily provided)
  • Business or enquiry-related details
  • Basic website usage data (analytics)

Platform-specific data (such as location, payments, or bookings) is handled by the respective brand platforms and their service providers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Responding to enquiries
  • Corporate administration
  • Legal and regulatory compliance

VG Corp does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

3. Data Sharing & Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect personal information. Data may be shared only where required for legal compliance or essential services.

Payment processing and user data for applications are managed by independent platforms and secure third-party providers.

4. Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to processing where applicable
